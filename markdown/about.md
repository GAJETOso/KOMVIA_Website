# About KOMVIA LIMITED

KOMVIA LIMITED is a private indigenous technology conglomerate duly registered in the Federal Republic of Nigeria under the **Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 2020**. Our mandate is to build the critical infrastructure that empowers sovereign nations and global enterprises through intelligent automation.

## Corporate Objects
In accordance with our Memorandum of Association, KOMVIA LIMITED is established for the following core mandates:

- **Provision of Innovative Technology Services**: Engineering next-generation software architectures for enterprise efficiency.
- **Digital & Data-driven Solutions**: Leveraging big data and neural intelligence to solve complex operational challenges.
- **General Contracts**: Managing large-scale technology deployments for public and private institutions.
- **General Merchandise**: Facilitating the global flow of commerce through intelligent supply chain ERP.

## Core Pillars

### Enterprise Intelligence
Our **KOMVIA ERP** suite provides a unified brain for operations, bringing high-fidelity predictive insights to global trade and local logistics.

### Financial Sovereignty
Through our **IFE Ecosystem**, we provide the rails for compliant, institutional payments integrated with the Pan-African Payment and Settlement System (PAPSS).

## Our Mission
To catalyze the digital transformation of Africa and the global south by providing technology that is secure, compliant, and optimized for profit and growth.
