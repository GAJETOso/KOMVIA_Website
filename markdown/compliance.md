# Compliance & Global Standards | KOMVIA LIMITED

KOMVIA LIMITED is committed to the highest levels of institutional integrity. Our ecosystem is hardened against cybersecurity threats and aligned with international financial and data regulations.

## 1. Data Protection (NDPR & GDPR)
We strictly adhere to the **Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR)** and the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**. Our data subjects have full control over their PII, protected by AES-256 encryption at rest.

## 2. Financial Standards (ISO 20022)
Our Integrated Financial Ecosystem (IFE) utilizes **ISO 20022** for all financial messaging, ensuring seamless interoperability with global banking networks and the Pan-African Payment and Settlement System (PAPSS).

## 3. Security Frameworks
- **ISO 27001 Alignment**: Information security management systems (ISMS) implementation.
- **PCI-DSS Layering**: Secured handling of cardholder data across all IFE rails.
- **SOC 2 Type II**: Operational excellence in security, availability, and processing integrity.

## 4. Regulatory Audit
KOMVIA undergoes routine internal and third-party audits to ensure our ERP and financial stacks meet the evolving requirements of the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) and other continental regulators.
