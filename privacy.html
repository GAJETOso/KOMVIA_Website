Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

At KOMVIA LIMITED ("KOMVIA", "we", "us", or "our"), your privacy and data security are cornerstones of our ecosystem. This policy outlines how we handle data across all our platforms, including KOMVIA ERP and KOMVIA IFE.

1. Data Collection & Usage

We process information to provide enterprise intelligence and financial services. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Full name, biometric identifiers (where applicable for KYC), and government IDs.
  • Financial Data: Transaction histories, wallet balances, and payment rail routing information.
  • Operational Data: ERP logs, inventory movements, and business intelligence metrics.

2. Institutional Security

We employ bank-grade security protocols, including AES-256 encryption for data at rest and mutual TLS (mTLS) for all inter-service communications. Our systems are routinely audited against ISO 27001 and PCI-DSS standards.

3. Your Rights

Under global data protection regulations (including GDPR and NDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your personal data processed within the KOMVIA ecosystem.

4. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@komvia.com.
