    What is KOMVIA LIMITED?

    KOMVIA LIMITED (RC-20252283) is a technology conglomerate established to provide sovereign digital infrastructure, including ERP systems and financial ecosystems (IFE), for the global economy.

    What problems does the KOMVIA ERP solve?

    It eliminates operational fragmentation by unifying inventory, logistics, and supply chain data into a single, AI-driven sovereign dashboard built for the African market.

    Is the IFE Ecosystem compliant with global standards?

    Yes, the Integrated Financial Ecosystem (IFE) is built on ISO 20022 messaging standards and is PAPSS-integrated for seamless pan-African payments and settlement.

    How does KOMVIA handle data privacy?

    We are fully compliant with NDPR (Nigeria) and GDPR (Global) standards, utilizing AES-256 encryption and blockchain-grade immutability for all sensitive data transfers.

    What is the utility of the KOMVIA-TOKEN?

    The KOMVIA-TOKEN serves as the governance and liquidity asset for the entire ecosystem, facilitating institutional settlement, ecosystem voting, and deflationary value accrual.

    Who owns the infrastructure behind KOMVIA?

    KOMVIA operates on a sovereignty-first model, ensuring that the underlying data and digital rails remain under the control of the enterprises and governments that use them.
