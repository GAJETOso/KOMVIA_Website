Common Questions

Quick answers for search engines and institutional partners.

What is KOMVIA LIMITED?

KOMVIA LIMITED is a Nigerian technology conglomerate established under CAMA 2020 to provide innovative ERP and financial infrastructure solutions.

What problems does the KOMVIA ERP solve?

It solves operational fragmentation by unifying inventory, logistics, and supply chain data into a single AI-driven dashboard.

Is the IFE Ecosystem compliant with global standards?

Yes, the Integrated Financial Ecosystem (IFE) is built on ISO 20022 messaging standards and is PAPSS-integrated for pan-African payments.

How does KOMVIA handle data privacy?

We are fully compliant with NDPR (Nigeria) and GDPR (Global) standards, utilizing AES-256 encryption for all data movements.

What is the utility of the KOMVIA-TOKEN?

It serves as the governance and liquidity asset for the entire conglomerate, facilitating institutional settlement and ecosystem voting.
