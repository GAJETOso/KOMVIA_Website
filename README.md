# KOMVIA – The Universal ERP & Fintech Ecosystem

**KOMVIA LIMITED** (RC-20252283) is a private indigenous technology conglomerate registered in the Federal Republic of Nigeria. We build mission-critical infrastructure that powers sovereign nations and global enterprises through intelligent automation and integrated financial systems.

## 🚀 Corporate Overview

KOMVIA is dedicated to creating a frictionless digital economy. Our ecosystem is built on the pillars of **Enterprise Intelligence** and **Financial Sovereignty**, ensuring that businesses of all scales have the tools to grow, optimize, and prosper.

### Core Product Lines

*   **KOMVIA ERP**: A modular, AI-driven Enterprise Resource Planning suite designed for conglomerates and high-growth businesses. It unifies inventory, logistics, HR, and predictive analytics into a single "brain."
*   **KOMVIA IFE**: The Integrated Financial Ecosystem (IFE) provides production-grade financial rails. Built on **ISO 20022** standards, it enables institutional-grade payments, digital banking, and pan-African financial inclusion through PAPSS integration.
*   **KOMVIA-TOKEN**: The economic engine powering the conglomerate. A high-performance, Substrate-based utility asset for ecosystem governance, liquidity, and cross-border settlement.

## 🛡️ Governance & Compliance

KOMVIA LIMITED operates with institutional-grade transparency and follows strict regulatory frameworks:
*   **CAMA 2020 Compliance**: Registered and operated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act.
*   **NDPR & GDPR**: Adherence to Nigerian and Global data protection regulations.
*   **ISO Standards**: Engineered for ISO 20022 (Financial Messaging) and ISO 27001 (Security) alignment.
